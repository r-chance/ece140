SWOT Analysis

Strengths

  • We are unique in that our product offers features that do not exist in the current market. Our pedals have internet connectivity unlike any other product. We create value by allowing users to save audio, profiles, and settings onto their account. We also offer customizable effects based on the orientation of the guitar.
  • Our product is better because it is wireless and allows the user to have more mobility.

    • Weaknesses

  • We do not have the same technical or business resources as our established competitors.

    • Opportunities

  • A trend right now is that people are getting bigger pedalboards and smaller pedals so that they can fit more pedals. This is an opportunity because it shows that people like to have more effects available to them, which is what our product is going to provide.
  • We have the opportunity to create a new social outlet within the guitar playing community by allowing users to save audio, profiles, and settings and share it with others.

    • Threats

  • An obstacle would be reaching consumers who are only interested in analog guitar pedals. Guitar players interested in the best sound quality (analog) would rather have many analog pedals than one digital pedal.
  • Getting users to learn how to use the pedal could be problematic. It would be slightly different than currently existing pedals, and not everyone is willing to learn to use something new when they have something that works for them.
