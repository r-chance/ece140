THE PROBLEM WE SEE AND OUR SOLUTION

The Problem

The problem is that music, especially produced by guitars, has not kept up with technology over the last couple of decades. The very minimal technology available to guitar players requires a lot of different expensive equipment to record audio and alter sounds.

The Solution

Our product solves the problem of requiring expensive equipment by offering a less expensive alternative. The Gator Pedal implements a simpler method and interconnectivity to make this technology more available and modern, while still being compatible with older equipment.
